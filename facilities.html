Lockouts

Lockouts

Residence Life staff, primarily the RAOD, will conduct lockouts between the hours of 7:00pm and 9:00am.

In the event the HDOD receives a call regarding a lock-out, the HDOD will call the RAOD and request response. If the RAOD is off-duty, the HDOD will instruct DPS to complete the lockout.

RHO Codes: Each RHO has a particular code that accesses the key box. In the event the HDOD needs access to the key box, he/she should contact the RAOD for the code.

Lockout Verification

  1. RAOD requests student’s GoCard or ID for verification of residency. An RA will not perform a lockout for anyone other than the listed occupants for the room.
  2. The RA will have the residence complete the lockout log located at the RHO. The resident should be notified of the fees (see below):

7:00pm-12:00am (midnight) = $5
12:00 am (midnight) – 9:00am = $10
9:00am – 10:15am = DPS responds to lockouts (No Fee)

  1. RAOD will request the fee in cash from the student.
  2. If student does not have cash, the RAOD will record the GoCard number and have the student sign indicating that they understand a fee will be charged to their student account.

The RAOD will then complete the lock-out form:

Emergency Lockchange

  1. Contact the Department of Public Safety and ask them to page the Maintenance II (M2) employee for emergency service. Identify yourself as a Senior Residence Life Staff member.
  2. The Senior Residence Life Staff and the resident must complete the Locksmith Services Release Form, which you can find in the HDOD binder.
  3. Contact the RA on duty for the building involved so the RA can meet the Maintenance II employee in the appropriate RHO to receive keys.
  4. Follow up by sending an e-mail to the Director of Housing the following day identifying the resident(s) to be charged.
  5. Hand-deliver the Locksmith Services Release Form to the attention of the Director of Housing, 100 Harbin Hall within two business days of the lock request.
